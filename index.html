What We May Lose

Indigenous languages carry culture, identity, history, and knowledge that cannot always be translated. Residential schools and assimilation policies interrupted language transmission across generations.

How This Campaign Elevates Voices

This website highlights Indigenous creators, Elders, language keepers, and organizations that help make language revitalization visible today. It connects history, research, and digital media to show that Indigenous languages are living languages.

What Communities Are Saving

Communities are saving words, stories, pronunciation, songs, and cultural knowledge through teaching, recording, family learning, and digital media.
