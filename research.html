What

Indigenous languages are deeply connected to identity, culture, and history.

Who

First Nations, Métis, Inuit communities, Elders, governments, and educators.

Where

Across Canada in schools, communities, and online platforms.

When

Language loss occurred over generations, especially during residential schools. TRC report (2015) and Indigenous Languages Act (2019).

Why

Language is essential for culture, identity, and reconciliation.
